## ğŸ“ OSCP Exam Tips

### Before the Exam
1. **Sleep well** - Get 8 hours before exam
2. **Prepare workspace** - Clean desk, good lighting
3. **Test VPN** - Connect 15 minutes early
4. **Have food/drinks ready** - Energy drinks, snacks
5. **Bathroom breaks planned** - Take them regularly

### During the Exam
1. **Read instructions carefully** - Know the rules
2. **Start with enumeration** - Don't rush
3. **Pick easiest first** - Build confidence
4. **Take notes** - Document everything
5. **Try Harder** - Reset if stuck for 2+ hours
6. **Sleep 4-6 hours** - In the middle of exam
7. **Don't panic** - You have 24 hours

### After Compromise
1. **Take screenshots** - `ipconfig/ifconfig`, `whoami`, proof.txt
2. **Backup shell** - Multiple access methods
3. **Save loot** - Credentials, hashes, files
4. **Move to next target** - Don't waste time

### Reporting
1. **Use template** - Save time
2. **Write as you go** - Document during exam
3. **Proofread** - Check for errors
4. **Submit early** - Don't wait until deadline
